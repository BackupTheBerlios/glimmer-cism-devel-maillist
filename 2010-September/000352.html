Thanks.  Note that this does not implement an exactly conservative
scheme.  See Table 4 which, after correcting for a total-area bias,
still shows errors typically larger than 10%.  This may be acceptable
for short time periods, but not for the long-term.

Also note that all the projections are readily available in the open
source library GDAL (http://gdal.org) which has bindings to many
languages and is much more modern, supported, and complete than Proj4
which some people are still using.
